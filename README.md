# **Excel Project Report: Vrinda Store Annual Sales Analysis 2022**

## **1. Project Overview**

This project focuses on analyzing the annual sales performance of Vrinda Store for the year 2022 using Microsoft Excel. The goal is to extract meaningful insights from sales data, understand customer behavior, and support business decision-making through data-driven analysis.

The project includes data cleaning, exploration, and visualization using Excel features such as Pivot Tables, Charts, and Dashboards.

---

## **2. Dataset Summary**

* **Total Rows:** 31,047
* **Total Columns:** 20

### **Key Features (Columns):**

* Order ID – Unique order identifier
* Customer ID – Unique customer reference
* Gender – Male/Female classification
* Age & Age Group – Customer demographics
* Date & Month – Order timeline
* Status – Order status (Delivered, Cancelled, Returned, etc.)
* Channel – Sales platform (Amazon, Flipkart, etc.)
* Category – Product category
* Size – Product size
* Quantity (Qty) – Units ordered
* Amount – Revenue generated
* Shipping Details – City, State, Postal Code, Country
* B2B – Business-to-business indicator

### **Missing Values:**

* No missing values were found in the dataset.
* The dataset is clean and ready for analysis.

---

## **3. Data Exploration**

Initial exploration was conducted to understand patterns and distributions:

* Orders are distributed across multiple months, indicating year-round sales activity.
* Customers belong to different age groups, allowing demographic segmentation.
* Multiple sales channels contribute to total revenue.
* Product categories and sizes vary, indicating diverse inventory.

---

## **4. Data Analysis Using Excel**

The following analyses were performed using Pivot Tables and Charts:

### **a. Sales vs Orders (Monthly Analysis)**

* Identified monthly trends in sales and order volume.
* Peak sales months were observed, indicating seasonal demand.

### **b. Gender-Based Sales**

* Women contributed a higher percentage of total sales compared to men.
* Indicates stronger engagement from female customers.

### **c. Order Status Analysis**

* Majority of orders were successfully delivered.
* A small percentage included cancellations and returns.

### **d. Sales by State**

* Certain states generated higher revenue, indicating strong regional markets.
* Helps identify top-performing geographic areas.

### **e. Age Group vs Sales**

* Adult age group (e.g., 25–45) contributed the most to sales.
* Younger and older segments contributed less comparatively.

### **f. Sales Channel Performance**

* Online marketplaces (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart) were major contributors.
* Channel comparison highlights best-performing platforms.

---

## **5. Business Recommendations**

Based on the analysis, the following recommendations are suggested:

1. **Target Female Customers**

   * Since women contribute more to sales, create targeted marketing campaigns and product offerings.

2. **Focus on Top-Performing States**

   * Increase inventory and promotions in high-performing regions to maximize revenue.

3. **Leverage High-Performing Channels**

   * Invest more in platforms that generate the most sales (e.g., Amazon, Flipkart).

4. **Seasonal Marketing Strategy**

   * Plan promotions during peak months to boost sales further.

5. **Improve Order Fulfillment**

   * Reduce cancellations and returns by improving product descriptions and logistics.

6. **Target Core Age Group**

   * Focus marketing on the adult demographic, which contributes the highest revenue.

## **Conclusion**

This Excel-based analysis provides valuable insights into customer behavior, sales performance, and market trends. The dashboard and visualizations help stakeholders quickly understand key metrics and make informed business decisions.
